Submitting to Jetty Powered and Jetty Case Studies

If you would like your product/project showcased on the Jetty Powered pages, then please send an email to powered@mortbay.com including the following information:
  • logo or icon in GIF, JPEG, PNG format
  • product/project summary
  • URL
If you would like to submit a Case Study article, then please send an email to casestudy@mortbay.com with:
  • The submission details listed above if you are NOT already listed as Jetty Powered
  • A synopsis of your article
  • Some background to your Jetty application, such as:
    • Do you use Jetty behind a traditional webserver, eg Apache?
    • Do you use Jetty standalone/embedded/ as part of a J2EE app server (specify)?
    • What version of Jetty do you currently use?
    • What version(s) of JVM do you currently use/support with Jetty?
    • What OS platform you deploy on/support?
    • Is your Jetty application commercial/not-for-profit/open source?
    • Is your Jetty application targetted at IT/telecoms/banking/education/other (specify)?
    • What kind of loads do you service with Jetty?
